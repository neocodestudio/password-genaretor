Internet Requirement

SecureGen requires an active internet connection (WiFi or Mobile Data) to operate. This serves two purposes: (1) it is a product-level design requirement for core functionality, and (2) it allows Google AdMob to load and display advertisements.

âœ…
Your Vault Data Is Never Transmitted

Even though an internet connection is required, SecureGen never sends, uploads, or syncs your vault data to any server. The connection is used only for connectivity detection and loading AdMob ads. You can verify this with a network traffic inspector â€” zero outbound vault data will be detected.

Connectivity is monitored using the connectivity_plus Flutter package. If no connection is detected, all core features are disabled and a clear message is shown until connectivity is restored.

Children's Privacy

SecureGen is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Because the App uses Google AdMob, we have taken additional steps to comply with child privacy laws:

COPPA

We have configured AdMob with child-directed treatment settings where applicable. This disables interest-based targeting, remarketing, and behavioural profiling for child users as required by the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act.

GDPR / TFUA

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) or UK under the age of digital consent, we have enabled the "tag for users under the age of consent in Europe" (TFUA) setting in AdMob. This disables personalised advertising for those users.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the App, please note that no vault data has been transmitted to us. For concerns about ad data collected by Google, review Google's family policies at families.google.com/familylink/privacy/child-policy or contact us directly.
